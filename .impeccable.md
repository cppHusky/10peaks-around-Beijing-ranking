## Design Context

### Users
The product serves two groups: administrators who upload and maintain xlsx source files for the Jingji Ten Peaks challenge ranking, and public visitors who only view and download the generated leaderboard.

### Brand Personality
The interface should feel efficient, calm, and trustworthy. It is a practical event operations tool first, with a public leaderboard that stays readable and neutral.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a minimal utility-oriented visual direction: clear tables, restrained controls, light backgrounds, high contrast, and little decoration. Avoid flashy event graphics or generic dashboard ornamentation.

### Design Principles
- Put data clarity before visual flourish.
- Keep administrator workflows direct and reversible.
- Make public browsing and downloading obvious without exposing private data.
- Use restrained color only for status, priority, and feedback.
